How It Works

TeqStories stands by you all the way to ensure that you achieve your learning goals.

Our technical Executive will get in touch with you on receiving your inquiry. He tries to understand all your learning need and clarify all your doubts. Our technical executive will share you the recorded session of your trainer. You go through the session to have a look and feel of the class. You can also have an interaction with the trainer if you have any queries.

You enroll for the course. You can enroll for a classroom or online medium. Content is shared across live screening. The trainer shows you practical live demonstration. You can always ask the trainer Incase of doubts and resolve it. Incase you miss a class you will have recorded session for your reference. You will also receive a full set of recording of the complete class on the enrollment for the course. You will have access to complete documentation and e-materials required for your learning.

The training is completely hands on and weekly assignment are given for better understanding. Incase of any doubts you can always reach out to our support team and we make sure that we resolve all your queries at lightening speed.

Post completion of the training you need to work on the live projects. Once your project is completed you need to get it reviewed. You can also schedule a consultation with the trainer post project completion. Get certified from TeqStories Your certificate holds a high value and the company can cross verify the same by logging in our portals.
